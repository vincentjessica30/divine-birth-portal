Maiden to Mother (or Mother Again) Package — $2000

A full pregnancy-to-postpartum support journey designed to guide mothers through preparation, birth, and the early weeks of motherhood with emotional, physical, and spiritual care.

This package includes:

Prenatal Connection & Pregnancy Support

Three prenatal sessions focused on preparation for birth and motherhood. Sessions may include childbirth education, discussion of common hospital interventions, comfort measures for labor, emotional support, empowerment practices, and birth plan creation.

On-Call Birth Support

Beginning at 37 weeks, 24/7 on-call availability for labor support.

Labor & Birth Support

Continuous in-person support during labor and birth, plus 1–2 hours of immediate postpartum care.

Postpartum Care

Five postpartum visits (4-hour sessions) focused on whatever support you need that day—recovery, nourishment, baby care guidance, emotional support, or rest.
